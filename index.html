ANNOUNCEMENTS
FOR RESIDENTSOF THE HOUSES

After you create such message it will be automatically sent to all residents of the multistory building. This will allow you to quickly distribute news and important information and save a lot of time for calls or sticking messages inside the building. This will be especially useful for buildings with plenty of flats.

ACCES TO
SECURITY CAMERAS

In the presence of security cameras in the multistory building, the user of our program can inspect areas where the cameras are installed. If the camera is located at the entrance, the user can watch the visitors.

THE STATE
OF THE METERS

A quick review of the status of all the meters that belong to the owner of the apartment in the application. If the user pays utilities through a special app or site, he can connect to them quickly by the our app.

A BRIEF NOTE
ON EACH INHABITANT

Every user will have a page, visible to all neighbors, which will contain a short list of his contact information ( email, phone number, social network page etc.)

NOTIFICATION
ABOUT ALARM

This feature will be available to residents of the home, which have the alarm system. The device will send a message about the alarm signal. This is especially useful for homes with high noise insulation.

MAIL
NOTFICATIONS

In the case of receiving any mail in the mailbox the inhabitant will receive a message, which will eliminate the need to check the mailboxes.
